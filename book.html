Want some pep at your next event?

The UC San Diego Pepband always interested in performing at new gigs!
We perform at a wide variety of events, including sports games, school events, local organzational events, and private/corporate events. We can only perform at venues located in the greater San Diego area, unless the event organizers are willing to provide us with travel accomodations.
To book us for your next event, fill out the Google form below. If we do not respond with 3 business days, please reach out to us via email(pepband@ucsd.edu) or Instagram(@ucsdpepband).
